Manifestation practice
15 minutes every morning at 9:30AM. Intention + emotion + one action.
1
Settle — 2 mins
Sit comfortably. Close your eyes. Take 5 slow deep breaths. Let the morning noise settle. You are not doing anything yet — just arriving in your own presence.
2
Write your intentions — 5 mins
3–5 statements in present tense, as if already true. Examples: "I am healthy and my body grows stronger each day." / "I am in a role that challenges and rewards me." / "My side projects generate income beyond my salary."
3
Visualise — 5 mins
Close your eyes. Pick one intention. See the specific moment it is real — a scene, not a vague future. Where are you? What does it feel like in your chest? Hold that feeling for 5 minutes. The emotion is the signal, not the picture.
4
Gratitude — 3 mins
3 genuine things. One must be small — your tea, the morning light, your own effort yesterday.

The one rule
Do not manifest and then wait. Every intention must have one action attached to it today — however small. Manifestation without action is daydreaming. You are building a life, not wishing for one.
